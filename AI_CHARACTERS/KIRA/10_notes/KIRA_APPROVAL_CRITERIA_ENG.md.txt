Purpose:
Quick checklist for deciding whether a generated Kira image should be approved, kept as candidate, or regenerated.

---

# 1. SIMPLE DECISION RULE

## APPROVE

Approve image if:

* she clearly looks like Kira;
* face matches Kira face canon;
* body matches Kira body canon;
* hair, eyes, freckles and general beauty type are preserved;
* body proportions are natural;
* outfit matches the intended canon;
* no serious anatomy errors.

## KEEP AS CANDIDATE

Keep as candidate if:

* image is good but not perfect;
* one part is useful, for example outfit, pose, dress or lighting;
* face is slightly weaker but body/outfit is good;
* body is slightly weaker but face/mood is good.

Candidate files should be stored in:

C:\DEV\Narrative\AI_CHARACTERS\KIRA\05_outfits\evening_dress\candidates\

or another relevant candidates folder.

## REGENERATE

Regenerate if:

* she looks like a different woman;
* face is wrong;
* body is wrong;
* bust becomes too small;
* legs become weak or too thin;
* glutes become flat;
* hair color changes too much;
* freckles / beauty mark disappear completely;
* anatomy is broken;
* image looks vulgar, fake, plastic, or not like Kira.

Bad or rejected files should be stored in:

C:\DEV\Narrative\AI_CHARACTERS\KIRA\07_generated\

---

# 2. BODY CHECK

Approve body if:

* athletic slender feminine body;
* natural final visual 3+ bust target;
* narrow waist;
* feminine hips;
* strong hurdler legs;
* firm glutes;
* not skinny fashion model;
* not bodybuilder;
* not crossfit-heavy.

Regenerate body if:

* bust is too small;
* legs are weak or thin;
* glutes are flat;
* waist becomes thick or boxy;
* body becomes too muscular;
* body becomes too dry fitness-stage look.

---

# 3. FACE CHECK

Approve face if:

* oval face;
* high cheekbones;
* expressive brown / amber eyes;
* dark blonde / light brown wavy shoulder-length hair;
* light freckles;
* small beauty mark above left upper lip;
* full lower lip;
* slight asymmetrical smile;
* warm, alive expression.

Regenerate face if:

* she looks like another woman;
* eyes are wrong color;
* hair becomes black, platinum, or flat straight;
* face becomes too plastic;
* freckles and beauty mark disappear;
* expression is dead or mannequin-like.

---

# 4. EVENING DRESS CHECK

Approve evening dress if:

* red fitted evening dress;
* elegant and tasteful;
* neckline is refined;
* waist, hips and bust read correctly;
* strong athletic leg is visible through slit;
* image is adult, cinematic, romantic, not vulgar.

Regenerate evening dress if:

* dress makes body flat;
* bust is reduced too much;
* waist/hips disappear;
* image becomes cheap glamour;
* face stops looking like Kira.

---

# 5. SPORTS CHECK

Approve sports look if:

* black fitted sports outfit;
* strong hurdler legs;
* firm glutes;
* narrow waist;
* athletic posture;
* face still looks like Kira;
* sports top compresses bust naturally but does not make her flat.

Regenerate sports look if:

* she becomes flat-chested;
* legs are too thin;
* glutes disappear;
* body becomes too bodybuilder-like;
* face becomes another woman.

---

# 6. FINAL STATUS TERMS

Use these labels:

APPROVED:
Good enough to be used as canon.

APPROVED CANDIDATE:
Good, but keep in candidates until final selection.

DRAFT:
Useful idea, but not canon.

REGENERATE:
Do not use. Generate again.

REJECTED:
Bad result. Store only in 07_generated if needed.
